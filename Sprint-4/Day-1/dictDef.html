Dictionary Definations:-

Ad hoc network

Ad hoc projects are projects that develop unexpectedly, typically when a problem arises. These types of projects are, therefore, not scheduled in advance. There are a few features that typically separate ad hoc projects from standard projects

Assistive technology

Assistive technology is any software or hardware you can use to help people who have various disabilities. Assistive technology products can include wheelchairs, prosthetics or text-to-speech technology.

Authentication

Authentication is how companies verify a person's identity or device before allowing them to access network systems. This cybersecurity strategy can protect an organization's sensitive information from unofficial access.
